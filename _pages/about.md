  <p style="text-align:justify">
Arafat Ibne Ikram is an Electrical Engineer, researcher, and industry professional with strong expertise in renewable energy systems, smart grids, and data-driven energy optimization. He completed his B.Sc. in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the International Islamic University Chittagong, where his undergraduate research focused on the techno-economic assessment of optimized hybrid microgrids. 
  </p>
  
  <p style="text-align:justify">
  He has worked as a Research Assistant under the supervision of Dr. M Shafiullah (KFUPM, Saudi Arabia), contributing to studies on renewable energy–integrated waste-to-energy systems, lifecycle cost analysis, and environmental impact assessment. His research has resulted in multiple peer-reviewed journal articles and international conference publications, including works in popular journals such as the Arabian Journal for Science and Engineering, IET Power Electronics, and The Journal of Engineering. He also serves as a peer reviewer for Energy Storage (Wiley), contributing to the evaluation of manuscripts in energy storage systems, optimization, and sustainable power technologies.
  </p>
  
  <p style="text-align:justify">
  Arafat has also served as a Teaching Assistant, mentoring undergraduate students in renewable energy modeling, MPPT-based solar PV systems, inverter control, and techno-economic evaluation using MATLAB and simulation tools. In parallel, he gained industry experience as a Data Analyst Associate at Catalyst Solutions, leading statistical analysis projects and developing data visualization dashboards for global clients. Currently, he works as a Maintenance Engineer (Electrical) at Park (Bangladesh) Company Ltd., where he is responsible for electrical system installation, maintenance, ensuring safety compliance, and providing operational support. His research interests lie at the intersection of sustainable energy systems, optimization algorithms, machine learning applications in power systems, and cost-effective low-carbon energy solutions.
  </p>
