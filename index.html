Biography

Arafat is a prospective graduate student, who is currently working under the supervision of Dr. Md Shafiullah. He is also working as teaching assistant under Engr. Md. Rashidul Islam at International Islamic University Chittagong. His research include hydrogen energy storage, metaheuristic approach of finding optimal sizing for microgrid, performance response of grid-integrated microgrid, power system protection, timeseries analysis for load forecasting, optimal load scheduling for minimum electricity cost. He received his bachelor degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering at International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh in 2022. Since his undergrad, he participates and contributes to several recent conference and so forth.
